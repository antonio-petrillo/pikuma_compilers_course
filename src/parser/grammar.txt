Grammar for mathematical expr:

<expr> ::= <term> ( <addop> <term>)*
<term> ::= <factor> ( <mulop> <factor>)*
<factor> ::= <unary>
<unary> ::= ('+', '-', '~') <unary> | <primary>
<primary> :: <number> | '(' <expr> ')'
<addop> ::= '+' | '-'
<mulop> ::= '*' | '/'
<number> ::= <digit>+
<digit> ::= [0-9]

Example: 2 + 42 * 2 + (47 * -21)

(+
    (+ 2
       (* 42 2))
    (* 47
       (- 21)))
